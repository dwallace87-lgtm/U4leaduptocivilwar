Timeline Background: Why Nat Turner Resisted

Context before we zoom in on each date

Background
Faith, oppression, and purpose

Nat Turner was an enslaved Black man in Virginia who grew up deeply religious. He believed God chose him to fight the violent system that allowed white enslavers to control every part of his life. Watching white power enforced by law and brutality convinced him that freedom would never come unless enslaved people resisted.

Keep this in mind during the timeline
  • Nat felt guided by visions and faith, not just anger.
  • He saw the rebellion as a moral mission against slavery.
  • Every event on the timeline shows how powerful the reaction was when enslaved people resisted.
